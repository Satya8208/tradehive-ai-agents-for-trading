Topic: Why meditation beats therapy for most people
Generated: 20260108_201948
========================================

1/ Therapy teaches you to understand your problems.

Meditation teaches you to stop identifying with them.

One analyzes the prisoner. The other realizes there's no cage.

2/ Therapy: "Let's explore why you feel anxious."

Meditation: "Who is watching the anxiety?"

> Therapy fixes the story > Meditation questions the storyteller

The watcher was never broken.

3/ Most therapy creates professional patients.

You become fluent in your damage. Expert at your triggers. Dependent on sessions for relief.

Meditation makes you your own guru.

4/ Therapy asks: "How do we solve this?"

Meditation asks: "Who has the problem?"

When you find the watcher, problems dissolve. Not because they're solved. Because there's no one left to have them.
