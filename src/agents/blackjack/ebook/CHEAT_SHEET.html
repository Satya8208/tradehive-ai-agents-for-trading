04 True Count Conversion

The Formula

True Count = Running Count ÷ Decks Remaining

Example: Running count is +6, about 2 decks left
True Count = 6 ÷ 2 = +3

Estimating Decks Remaining

Look at the discard tray:

  • 1 deck = about 1 inch of cards
  • 6-deck shoe starts with ~6 inches
  • If 2 inches in discard, ~4 decks left

06 5-Step Quick Start

1

Master Basic Strategy First

Learn the chart above until decisions are automatic. This alone cuts house edge to ~0.5%.

2

Practice Counting at Home

Flip through a deck, keeping count. Target: under 30 seconds with zero errors.

3

Learn True Count Conversion

Running count ÷ decks remaining. Practice estimating decks from the discard tray.

4

Practice with Distractions

Count while watching TV or with music. The casino will be noisy — train for it.

5

Start Small at Real Tables

Begin at low-stakes tables. Focus on counting accurately before betting big.
