**Subject:** "The dealer is hot" — biggest myth in gambling

**Body:**

I hear this constantly:

"The dealer is hot tonight. I'm going to move to another table."

Let me be clear: **This is complete nonsense.**

The dealer doesn't control the cards. They don't have a magic touch. They're just doing their job — flipping cards in a predetermined order.

When someone says the dealer is "hot," what they mean is:

"Recent results have been bad for players at this table."

But here's the thing about randomness:

**Past results don't predict future results.**

The cards don't know what happened before. Every shuffle is a reset.

What DOES matter:
- Deck penetration (how far into the shoe they deal)
- Number of decks
- Table rules (3:2 vs 6:5 blackjack)
- Your current count

These are facts. "Hot dealer" is superstition.

If you're making table decisions based on dealer streaks, you're playing a losing game within a losing game.
