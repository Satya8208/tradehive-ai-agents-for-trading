Full-table running count — build speed and accuracy

How It Works

Cards are dealt to 5-6 players plus the dealer. Your job is to maintain the running count as each card appears. The drill shows all cards for the configured speed, then asks for your count. Hi-Lo system: Low cards (2-6) = +1, High cards (10-A) = -1, 7-9 = 0.

Bet Sizing Fundamentals

When the True Count is high, you have an edge and should bet big. When TC is negative or low, bet the minimum. The spread ratio (max bet / min bet) determines how aggressively you press your advantage. Typical spreads range from 8:1 to 16:1 depending on penetration.
